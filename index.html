Dashboard Purpose

  • Identify counties and regions performing above or below the NYS statewide average.
  • Monitor low birth weight and very low birth weight trends from 2008 to 2023.
  • Trace birth weight flows from category to region to performance band using the Sankey diagram.
  • Compare regional deviation from NYS averages across all three birth weight metrics using the diverging bar chart.
  • Flag high-risk counties by LBW rate and track improvement or worsening since 2008.
  • Support public health planning and program targeting using interactive county-level trend data.

Birth Weight Category → Region → Performance Band

Sankey diagram showing how live births flow from birth weight categories to NYS regions and then to performance bands for the selected year. Performance bands compare each region with the statewide average.

Regional Summary
Performance Band Rules
Better: better than NYS average by at least 0.30 percentage points.
Similar: within ±0.30 percentage points of NYS average.
Worse: worse than NYS average by at least 0.30 percentage points.
For Normal Birth Weight, higher is better. For Low and Very Low Birth Weight, lower is better.

Regional Performance vs. NYS Average

Diverging bars show each region's deviation (in percentage points) from the statewide average for the selected year. Bars extending right = above NYS avg · bars extending left = below NYS avg. Direction of "good" differs by metric.
